Jike Zhong

I am an engineer with the data science team at Salesforce working on LLM applications for forecasting and planning services.

Previously I obtained my bachelor's degrees from Ohio State University in both Computer Science and Data Analytics (Statistics). I was very fortunately advised by Prof. Wei-Lun Chao and I worked closely with Dr. Hong-You Chen. I also spent some wonderful time as a visiting research fellow at CCVL lab at Johns Hopkins University supervised by Bloomberg Distinguished Professor Dr. Alan Yuille

My current research interest centers around the adaptation, robustness, and reliability of foundation models (VLM, LLM, etc). In a broader term, I'm interested in improving our society with ML, especially focusing on the problems that can be resolved using clean, reliable, and deployable learning-based solutions. With this goal, I have worked on federated learning, weakly supervised learning, and self-supervised learning during my undergrad.

Please reach out if you have any ideas to discuss or just wanna have a chat! Last update: 04/2023
